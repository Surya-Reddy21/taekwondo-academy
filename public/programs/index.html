Programs for Every Age & Level

Ages 4 – 7

🐯 Little Tigers

Where champions begin.

Our Little Tigers program is specially designed for young children who are just discovering movement, coordination, and social skills. Through games, music, and beginner Taekwondo techniques, we make every class an adventure while quietly building focus, listening, and respect.

<h4>What They'll Learn:</h4>
<ul>
  <li>Basic kicks, stances, and blocks</li>
  <li>Following instructions and class etiquette</li>
  <li>Stranger danger and personal safety concepts</li>
  <li>Teamwork, turn-taking, and positive reinforcement</li>
  <li>Belt progression with age-appropriate grading</li>
</ul>

  <div class="program-meta-item"><strong>Class Size:</strong> Max 12 students</div>
  <div class="program-meta-item"><strong>Duration:</strong> 45 minutes</div>
  <div class="program-meta-item"><strong>Uniform:</strong> Provided on enrollment</div>
  <div class="program-meta-item"><strong>Belt Range:</strong> White → High Yellow</div>
</div>

Ages 8 – 14

⚡ Junior Champions

Build confidence. Compete. Excel.

Junior Champions is our flagship kids' program. Students follow the full Taekwondo curriculum — poomsae (forms), sparring, board breaking, and character development. Regular belt gradings provide clear milestones, and tournament participation is encouraged for those who want to compete.

<h4>What They'll Learn:</h4>
<ul>
  <li>Full WTF/WT poomsae curriculum through colored belts</li>
  <li>Olympic-style sparring with full protective gear</li>
  <li>Breaking techniques (board & rebreakable boards)</li>
  <li>Self-defense scenarios appropriate for their age</li>
  <li>Goal-setting, leadership, and mental toughness</li>
</ul>

<div class="program-meta-grid">
  <div class="program-meta-item"><strong>Class Size:</strong> Max 20 students</div>
  <div class="program-meta-item"><strong>Duration:</strong> 60 minutes</div>
  <div class="program-meta-item"><strong>Gear:</strong> Sparring gear required at red belt</div>
  <div class="program-meta-item"><strong>Belt Range:</strong> White → Red Belt</div>
</div>

Ages 15+

🔥 Teen & Adult Program

No experience needed. Just the will to start.

Our adult program welcomes complete beginners and experienced martial artists alike. Classes balance technical skill development, fitness, and competition preparation. Adults especially appreciate the stress-relieving power of a focused workout that also sharpens the mind.

<h4>What You'll Get:</h4>
<ul>
  <li>Full Taekwondo curriculum from white belt to black belt</li>
  <li>High-intensity sparring and conditioning sessions</li>
  <li>Poomsae training for grading and competition</li>
  <li>Flexibility, core strength, and cardiovascular fitness</li>
  <li>A welcoming community of like-minded adults</li>
</ul>

All Ages · Invitation

🏆 Black Belt Program

The pinnacle of your martial arts journey.

Invitation-only for dedicated color-belt students demonstrating exceptional commitment. The Black Belt Program provides advanced poomsae, deeper sparring strategy, referee and leadership training, and preparation for national tournaments. This is where champions are refined.

<h4>Program Highlights:</h4>
<ul>
  <li>Advanced poomsae: Koryo, Keumgang, Taebaek and beyond</li>
  <li>National and international competition preparation</li>
  <li>Leadership and junior instructor opportunities</li>
  <li>Strength & conditioning tailored for competitive athletes</li>
  <li>Direct mentorship from Master [Instructor Name]</li>
</ul>

<div class="program-meta-grid">
  <div class="program-meta-item"><strong>Entry Requirement:</strong> Red/Black belt or instructor invite</div>
  <div class="program-meta-item"><strong>Duration:</strong> 90 minutes</div>
  <div class="program-meta-item"><strong>Schedule:</strong> Separate timetable</div>
  <div class="program-meta-item"><strong>Belt Range:</strong> 1st Dan and above</div>
</div>

Ages 12+

🛡️ Self-Defense Workshop

Real skills for real situations.

This monthly workshop series focuses on practical self-defense drawn from Taekwondo, with emphasis on awareness, de-escalation, and effective techniques anyone can use. Great as a standalone course or as a supplement to regular training.

<h4>Topics Covered:</h4>
<ul>
  <li>Situational awareness and threat assessment</li>
  <li>Escapes from grabs, holds, and common attacks</li>
  <li>Verbal de-escalation strategies</li>
  <li>Safety for children: stranger awareness module</li>
  <li>Confidence-building drills with a partner</li>
</ul>
