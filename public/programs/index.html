Programs for Every Age & Level

World-class training tailored to where you are — and where you want to go.

Ages 4 – 7

🐯 Little Tigers

Where champions begin.

Our Little Tigers program is specially designed for young children who are just discovering movement, coordination, and social skills. Through games, music, and beginner Taekwondo techniques, we make every class an adventure while quietly building focus, listening, and respect.

What They'll Learn:

  • Basic kicks, stances, and blocks
  • Following instructions and class etiquette
  • Stranger danger and personal safety concepts
  • Teamwork, turn-taking, and positive reinforcement
  • Belt progression with age-appropriate grading
Class Size: Max 12 students
Duration: 45 minutes
Uniform: Provided on enrollment
Belt Range: White → High Yellow

Ages 8 – 14

⚡ Junior Champions

Build confidence. Compete. Excel.

Junior Champions is our flagship kids' program. Students follow the full Taekwondo curriculum — poomsae (forms), sparring, board breaking, and character development. Regular belt gradings provide clear milestones, and tournament participation is encouraged for those who want to compete.

What They'll Learn:

  • Full WTF/WT poomsae curriculum through colored belts
  • Olympic-style sparring with full protective gear
  • Breaking techniques (board & rebreakable boards)
  • Self-defense scenarios appropriate for their age
  • Goal-setting, leadership, and mental toughness
Class Size: Max 20 students
Duration: 60 minutes
Gear: Sparring gear required at red belt
Belt Range: White → Red Belt

Ages 15+

🔥 Teen & Adult Program

No experience needed. Just the will to start.

Our adult program welcomes complete beginners and experienced martial artists alike. Classes balance technical skill development, fitness, and competition preparation. Adults especially appreciate the stress-relieving power of a focused workout that also sharpens the mind.

What You'll Get:

  • Full Taekwondo curriculum from white belt to black belt
  • High-intensity sparring and conditioning sessions
  • Poomsae training for grading and competition
  • Flexibility, core strength, and cardiovascular fitness
  • A welcoming community of like-minded adults
Class Size: Max 25 students
Duration: 75 minutes
Fitness Level: All welcome
Belt Range: White → Black Belt

The pinnacle of your martial arts journey.

Program Highlights:

Ages 12+

🛡️ Self-Defense Workshop

Real skills for real situations.

This monthly workshop series focuses on practical self-defense drawn from Taekwondo, with emphasis on awareness, de-escalation, and effective techniques anyone can use. Great as a standalone course or as a supplement to regular training.

Topics Covered:

  • Situational awareness and threat assessment
  • Escapes from grabs, holds, and common attacks
  • Verbal de-escalation strategies
  • Safety for children: stranger awareness module
  • Confidence-building drills with a partner
Format: Monthly workshop (2 hours)
