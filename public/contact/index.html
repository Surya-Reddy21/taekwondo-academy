Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Do I need any experience to join?

Absolutely not. We welcome complete beginners of all ages. Your first class is free with no commitment.

❓ What should I wear to my first class?

Comfortable sportswear (tracksuit, shorts, t-shirt) is fine for your trial class. Once you enroll, we'll help you get the right uniform (dobok).

❓ How long does it take to get a black belt?

For a dedicated student training 2–3 times per week, the average time is 4–5 years. It varies by age, commitment, and natural progress.

❓ Is Taekwondo safe for young children?

Yes — with proper supervision and age-appropriate instruction, Taekwondo is one of the safest martial arts for children. Our Little Tigers classes are non-contact and focus on fun fundamentals.

❓ Do you offer family discounts?

Yes! We offer sibling and family membership discounts. Contact us for details.

❓ What if I miss a class?

We offer make-up classes for missed sessions. Just let your instructor know in advance when possible.
