What to Expect at Your First Class

1

Book Your Spot

Call us, fill in the contact form, or just walk in during any class time. We'll confirm the best class for your age and level.

2

Arrive 10 Minutes Early

Come in comfortable sportswear — tracksuit, shorts, t-shirt. No shoes needed on the mat. Bring a water bottle.

3

Meet Your Instructor

Your instructor will introduce you to the class, explain the etiquette, and ensure you feel comfortable from the very first bow.

4

Train & Decide

Join in, have fun, and take your time deciding. There is absolutely no pressure to enroll on the day.
